## 🚀 About Me

I am a final-year AI and Data Science engineering student at TEK-UP University, finishing my degree in June 2026. I have been building real projects for over three years across machine learning, cybersecurity and full-stack development. Right now I am doing my end-of-study internship at Talan Tunisie Consulting, working on QuantumGuard, a research project that explores how a reinforcement learning agent can perform dynamic quantum error correction using Qiskit. I enjoy working at the intersection of research and implementation and I am looking to pursue a PhD in AI or quantum computing after graduating.

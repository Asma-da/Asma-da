## 🧠 Who Am I?

<img align="right" alt="coding" width="350" src="https://media.giphy.com/media/qgQUggAC3Pfv687qPC/giphy.gif"/>

I am a final-year AI and Data Science engineering student at TEK-UP University, graduating in June 2026. Over the past three years I have built real systems across machine learning, cybersecurity, quantum computing and full-stack development — not just academic projects but things that actually work.

Right now I am doing my end-of-study internship at **Talan Tunisie Consulting**, working on **QuantumGuard** — a research project that trains a reinforcement learning agent to observe and dynamically correct errors in quantum circuits using Qiskit. It sits at the boundary between AI and quantum computing, which is exactly where I want to be.

After graduating I am looking to pursue a **PhD at the intersection of AI and quantum computing**, and I am planning to publish a paper on QuantumGuard once the research is complete.

When I am not building things, I am leading cybersecurity events, competing in CTFs, or diving into the latest research papers on quantum error correction and generative AI.

## 🚀 What I Am Working On

> **QuantumGuard** — End-of-study research project at Talan Tunisie Consulting.
> A reinforcement learning agent that learns to detect error patterns in quantum circuits and adaptively applies quantum error correction codes (repetition code and surface code) using Qiskit, without human supervision. The goal is to push toward more fault-tolerant quantum systems using AI.
